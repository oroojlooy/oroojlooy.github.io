Hi there, I'm Afshin Oroojlooy!

I am a forth year PhD student of Industrial Engineering at Lehigh University . I got my M.S. of Industrial Engineering from Sharif University of Technology and B.S of Industrial Engineering from Isfahan University of Technology. My research is on the application of learning algorithms---specially Reinforcement Learning---in supply chain problem problems., e.g. inventory optimization on multi-echelon networks, stochastic vendor route problem, customer journey optimization, etc. Read more about my research...
